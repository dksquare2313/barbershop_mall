About

Who we are

Forming strong, long-term relationships with each of our clients is our primary goal. Our services includes:

  • Professional Service.
  • Wide varity of styles.
  • Experience that speak by its work.

Each haircut and hot lather shave performed by our unmatched staff of skilled barbers is something they take personally. No employee at Shaving Grace has less than ten years of experience shaving. Everything has been seen and done by us. Our focus is on talent and substance, not tricks. Although we enjoy ourselves, we also take our work seriously.

Better yet, see us in person!

We love our customers, so feel free to visit during normal business hours.
